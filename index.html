Fix broken captive portals

Hotel wifi not working? Can't log on to free public wifi?

Just by visiting this site you should be redirected to log in to the captive portal

How?

  • Captive portals need to redirect you so you can log in.
  • SSL/TLS/https (the padlock symbol) prevents this.
  • This site will never use those technologies.
  • The captive portal can now redirect your connection...
  • ...and hopefully you can login.
